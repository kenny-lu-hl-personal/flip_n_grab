How to Play

Quick reflexes needed! This is a clone of the card game Jungle Speed. Win by being the first player to get rid of all your cards.

Players take turns revealing the top card of their deck. When a player reveals a card that matches another player's card, both players grab the totem placed in the center of the table. The faster player then gives their cards to their unfortunate opponent.

Checkout the Jungle Speed rulebook for the complete rules.
